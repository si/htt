Award Winning Hitting the Target

Educational game for learning angles through sports

Hitting the Target is an educational game created by Simon Jobling for learning angles in mathematics through different sports. Learn about measuring and estimating angles with snooker, right angles in football, acute angles in tennis and obtuse angles in cricket. The principles of learning mathematics conform to Key Stage 2 guidelines in the UK.
